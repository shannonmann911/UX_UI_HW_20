Overview

Sound OFF is an app that helps parents feel at ease and in the know while their child is away at camp and feel like the are in the know with daily check-ins, photos, and even mail call!

The Team

Shannon Mann
Candace Clay

My Role

Logo Design, User Research, Interaction Designer, Prototyper

The Problem

Our team has observed that parents feel uneasy about sending their children to summer camp, knowing there will be no communication with them for a week. Sound OFF is an app designed for parents to indirectly communicate with their campers. We believe that providing an app for parents to check-in with their children at summer camp will allow parents to feel at ease.

User Interviews

Over a 3 week period we surveyed 5 parents ages 33-45 who sent their child to summer camp for the first time this summer.
